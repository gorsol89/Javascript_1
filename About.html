Our Story

Welcome to RainyDays, where we believe in pushing the comfort zone! Founded by Theo Rain and Theresa Day in the charming town of Jacketville, our mission is to keep you cozy and stylish, no matter the weather.

It all started on a particularly drizzly day when Theo and Theresa, two rain enthusiasts with a knack for fashion, decided to create a line of jackets that would make people look forward to rainy days. Combining functionality with flair, our jackets are designed to withstand the elements while making a statement.

From the bustling streets of Jacketville to wherever you are, RainyDays is dedicated to providing top-notch jackets that blend comfort, durability, and style. So, embrace the rain, push the comfort zone, and step out in confidence with RainyDays!
